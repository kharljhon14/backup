Selection Guide

By default, the selected option is Territories. You can then choose one or more Divisions associated with that territory.

Once divisions are selected, the Location dropdown will be enabled, allowing you to choose one or more locations linked to those divisions.

Remove selections using the remove buttons inside each dropdown.
